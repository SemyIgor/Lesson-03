## Data types:

1. Integer
   - int;
   - from -2'147'483'648 to 2'147'483'647;
   - 32-bit number negative & positive;
2. Double
   - double;
   - from +-5,0x10-324 to +-1,7x10308;
   - 64-bit number negative & positive;
3. String
   - string;
   - from 0 to a lot off;
   - 4 byte to 2 Gbyte;
4. Boolean
   - bool;
   - true & false;
   - 1 byte;
